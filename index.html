Unity/Game Development

Cyber Apes

Cyberapes is a game development project of two friends and myself. We are developing the game using Unity and C#. Although none of us would consider himself an artist, we are still trying to do most of the Design stuff on our own. The game is a 4 Player Free for all multiplayer game, in which you have to try to obtain all 4 power items in order to escape the map. During the developement cycle we are also focusing on implementing a reinforcement KI so people without friends can have the same great experience.
